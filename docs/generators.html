6 Generators

Generators create audible sound. Chapter 4 introduced oscillators, which are the most widely used generator. This chapter expands upon oscillators and how they can be combined together to produce fuller and more interesting sounds. Two new generators are also introduced, noise and samplers. Noise can likewise be combined with oscillators and other generators to create richer and more realistic sound. Samplers represent the extreme of realism in generators, at least as far as their digital representation allows.
